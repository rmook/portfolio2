Zoo Membership

A Louisville Zoo Membership gives you a FULL YEAR of excitement and adventure including parking and unlimited FREE admission during regular Zoo hours. Come every week or everyday! It's your Frequent Fun Pass!

Benefits:

We’re open year-round for Zoo Members to enjoy the following benefits:

  • UNLIMITED FREE admission for 12 full months from purchase date including Papa John's Splash Park (seasonal attraction) every time you visit during regular hours
  • PARKING during regular Zoo hours is included in all membership levels (excludes select after-hours special events)
  • DISCOUNTS on education classes and camps; regular general admission for extra guests; ride/attraction packages and gift shop and concession purchases
  • SPECIAL PRICING to select special events including "The World’s Largest Halloween Party!” presented by Meijer and Santa's Safari. (Limited tickets available per day.)
  • ZOOBUCK COUPONS valid for discounts on admission, concessions, and select rides and attractions as well as community activities. Available through quarterly FREE Trunkline Magazine
  • 50% admission to 125 reciprocal zoos and aquariums. Click here for current reciprocal list
  • SPECIAL OFFERS to Newport Aquarium and other partner attractions. Click here for details.
  • E-NEWSLETTERS with news and events plus SPECIAL OFFERS
  • Memberships are TAX-DEDUCTIBLE to the extent provided by law

Benefits and prices subject to change without notice.
